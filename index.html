How to Tie a Shoe in 5 Steps!

Today, you will learn how to tie your shoe in 5 easy steps. Once you learn this, you will be able to tie your shoes like a pro and have no untied laces. There are multiple styles of tying a shoe, and you will learn one of the most common ones. It's known as the bunny ear technique, and it's an easy one to learn and remember.


Step 1: Get the Shoe(s)

Image made by Spencer Call, date created: 10/15/2024

Before you start tying, you first must get a shoe or pair of shoes. Don't get ones that don't have laces or don't need tying. You could use a different kind of footwear like boots instead as long as they have laces and need tying.


Step 2: Tie a Simple Knot

Image made by Spencer Call, date created: 10/15/2024

Now, you must tie a simple knot. You take the two shoelace ends and make a X with them. Then put one of those ends under and pull both ends. That's how you get a simple knot. Make sure it's tight enough so it isn't won't be loose later. You don't want to have any shoe untied so fast while walking or running.


Step 3: Make Bunny Ears

Image made by Spencer Call, date created: 10/15/2024

The bunny ear technique isn't called that for nothing. Now, you will take each of the lace ends again. With each end, you are going to make a loop. Make sure they are big enough for your shoe so you won't have many problems with tying.


Step 4: Making a Knot with the Loops/Bunny Ears

Image made by Spencer Call, date created: 10/15/2024

This step will be simlar to step 2 of these instructions. You will make a X with each lace end, but you will keep holding the loops/bunny ears. Then, you will put one of the ends under and over. Then get read to pull and check the loops to see they are fine


Step 5: Pull and Done

Image made by Spencer Call, date created: 10/15/2024

The final step is to pull the ends tightly then you are done. Congrats, you have learned how to tie a shoe. If you keep practicing, you will be a pro at this and be able to tie faster and better. Also, you could do a double knot if you want to tie your shoes more seurely. All you have to do is to repeat steps 4-5 using the already made bunny ears to make another knot. Thank you for using these instrustions, and have a good day.
